Defined Benefit Pension and Nonpension Postretirement Benefit Plan Financial Information
The following tables through page 108 represent financial information for the company's retirement-related benefit plans, excluding
DC plans. The DB pension plans under U.S. Plans consist of the Qualified PPP, the Excess PPP and the Retention Plan. The DB
pension plans and the nonpension postretirement benefit plans under non-U.S. Plans consist of all plans sponsored by the
company's subsidiaries. The nonpension postretirement benefit plan under U.S. Plan consists of only the U.S. Nonpension
Postretirement Benefit Plan.
