Fixed assetsFixed assets appearing on AFD’s balance sheet include property, plant and equipment and intangible assets. Fixed assets are recorded at their acquisition cost plus directly similar expenses.If a fixed asset consists of a number of items that may be regularly replaced and have different useful lives, each item is booked separately according to its own depreciation table. This item-by-item approach has been used for the building of the registered office.
