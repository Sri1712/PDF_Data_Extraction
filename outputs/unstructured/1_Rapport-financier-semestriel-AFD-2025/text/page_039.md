It acts primarily through investments in investment funds, as this activity enables it to increase the impact of its financing by supporting a large number of companies doing business in multiple sectors, thus promoting economic growth and the creation of job-creating businesses.AFD Group also holds direct equity investments with put options for operational purposes.The contractual flows of these financial assets are not SPPI and are therefore measured at fair value through profit and loss.Note 1.3 – Equity instruments at fair value through profit and lossEquity instruments measured at fair value through profit and loss correspond to investments held by AFD for which the classification at fair value through other comprehensive income which may not be recycled has not been selected.The Group has opted for a classification at fair value through other comprehensive income which may not be recycled for its portfolio of direct equity investments without put options, which
